Reflection: Life in the Quaint Human Preserve

The robots and AIs have taken over and placed humanity in the Quaint Human Preserve.

The preserve looks just like our world does now, except you no longer need money to get things. Food, housing, entertainment, travel, and basic needs are all handled for you.

Prompt:

How would you occupy your time?

Your answer can be funny, serious, practical, weird, optimistic, or suspicious. Explain what you would spend your days doing and why.
